Farming Tips & Best Practices

⛅ Weather Tips

  • Rainy Season: Prepare fields for drainage and plant water-loving crops like rice
  • Summer: Use drip irrigation systems and mulching to retain soil moisture
  • Winter: Plant cold-hardy crops like wheat, peas, and carrots
  • Spring: Best time for vegetable cultivation and crop rotation

🥗 Organic Fertilizer Tips

  • Cow Dung: Excellent for soil improvement and slow nutrient release
  • Compost: Use well-decomposed compost 2-3 months before planting
  • Green Manure: Plant legumes in off-season to fix nitrogen naturally
  • Neem Cake: Natural pest repellent and soil conditioner

🌱 Seasonal Suggestions

  • Monsoon (June-September): Rice, cotton, and pulses
  • Winter (October-March): Wheat, gram, barley, and mustard
  • Summer (April-May): Maize, vegetables, and groundnut
  • Year-Round: Turmeric, ginger, and aromatic crops

💧 Water Management

  • Soil Testing: Test soil moisture before irrigation
  • Drip Irrigation: Saves 30-40% water compared to flood irrigation
  • Rainy Season: Collect rainwater for dry season use
  • Timing: Early morning or evening irrigation reduces evaporation
